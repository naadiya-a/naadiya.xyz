We are proud to be the first salon in Vancouver to embrace the Deva concept in cutting and styling. For more than half a decade, we have been building relationships with curly haired women throughout the lower mainland to take curly girl fear out of the salon experience. By incorporating the Deva technique and philosophy with our many years of experience, we have become the go-to salon for curls. We celebrate all textures from soft waves to the tightest curls, and we offer all of the take home support products and tips necessary for you to easily maintain your hair at home.
